What I'm doing now

Writing

Everyday I write, for my job or for fun. I haven't gone a day without writing in three years. I don't really have any goals for my writing anymore. Maybe I will eventually. Now, I just write for the sake of writing.

Reading

I read to learn. Some of the smartest people in the world spend their lives learning about a subject, then they put it all down in a book. I'm so thankful for that. I'd like to start reading more fiction.

Learning

I'm a curious person. I know very little. I want to soak up as much as I can. Other than books, I love watching YouTube videos and hounding smart people with questions about everything I know nothing about. Lately, I'm most curious about world history and space.

Hobbies

Lifting weights, yoga, hiking, guitar, writing, improv, and watching standup comedy. Always trying new things too.
